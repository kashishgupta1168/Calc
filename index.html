Mahatma Gandhi

BIOGRAPHY

"Father of Indian Nation."

The person who taught us to fight making non-voilence and patience as his weapon.

  • It was 2 October 1869 Saturday, when Mahatma Gandhi was born in Porbandar, Gujarat.
  • He was raised by Karamchand Gandhi and Putli Bai.
  • At the early age of 13, he was married to Kasturba Gandhi.
  • Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi was a great freedom fighter of India.
  • He started Non-Cooperation Movement against Britishers.
  • Truth and non-violence was his major weapon.
  • He led Salt March, Harijan Movement, Kheda, and Champaran Movement in India.He spread the message of equality and brotherhood in society.
  • In Delhi, Nathuram Godse fired three bullets on him. It was 30 January 1948 Friday, when Gandhi Ji left us at the age of 78.
